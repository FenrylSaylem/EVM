How does it work? What is happening? If you decide to charge your car and provide flexibility you will be rewarded!
When you confirm the settings we're calculating the difference between your current State of Charge and the Target State of Charge. For this difference you pay the normal fees.

Whenever the minimum state of charge is reached your electronic vehicle battery will be used to support the electrical grid to flatten the peaks in demand and oversaturation.

But at most going up to your maximum state of charge.
For providing that flexibility you will be rewarded with EVToken.

If you have to leave in a case of emergency, you will only be charged for the difference between the starting charge and the current charge.
